HubSpot Use Cases and Deployment Scope

As inbound marketers we use HubSpot to run our inbound campaigns, from landing pages with CTAs and forms, to using the automation tools for our lead nurturing emails.

We are also HubSpot Partners and promote the tool to our own clients. The clients that have bought into HubSpot have seen major results using this great tool along with ourselves in the company.

HubSpot touches many of our departments. Predominately our Inbound Department, but also Sales, and Development.

The HubSpot social media tool is also one of the best marketing automation tools I have used, the reporting is great and completely customised.

HubSpot Pros

  • Everything you need is in one place, no need for other tools
  • Quick and easy to set up, support is there if you need it too from the guys at HubSpot
  • Integrates with a lot of other pieces of software and tools that I use which makes collaborating much easier.

HubSpot Cons

  • In terms of its social media section, they need to update their features and when new features are released on social media channels such as Twitter.
  • So many different options and features to use, you can sometimes get lost in the noise.

Likelihood to Recommend HubSpot

10.0

Perfect product for a business wanting to move all of their sales and marketing into one place. Great for social media, couldn't recommend it more.
