Still needs a lot of development

We've been using HubSpot as our CRM for the past year and a half. We're a SaaS startup.
Many HubSpot features, especially on the marketing side, are great. Attribution reports and contact timelines are useful ways to see where contacts are coming from and what they see before converting. Workflows are powerful and let me keep information organized.

However, the HubSpot CRM / Sales side doesn't get much love. It's not sophisticated enough to track all the complicated relationships we have with people in our database. For example, the only available lifecycle stages are Lead, Marketing Qualified Lead, SQL, Customer, Evangelist, and Other. What do we do with the contacts who are our team members? Investors? Business partners?
Other missing features, just to name a few:
- Deal Pipeline view lacks crucial properties like "last emailed"
- No bulk email
- Reports shouldn't be an expensive add-on, should be included like in Salesforce.
- The Marketing email builder is frequently buggy.
